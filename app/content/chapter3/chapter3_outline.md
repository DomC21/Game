# Chapter 3: Technical Analysis Essentials

## Chapter Overview
This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts and tools of technical analysis for trading and investing. Technical analysis is the study of historical price and volume data to forecast future price movements. By mastering these essential techniques, traders can identify trends, recognize patterns, and make more informed trading decisions based on market behavior rather than fundamental factors.
