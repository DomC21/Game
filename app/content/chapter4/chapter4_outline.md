# Chapter 4: Fundamental Analysis & Valuation

## Chapter Overview
While technical analysis (Chapter 3) helps you gauge market sentiment through price and volume patterns, fundamental analysis aims to assess a company's intrinsic value by examining its financial statements, industry conditions, and economic factors. In this chapter, we'll dive into key financial metrics, valuation methods, and qualitative factors that can guide long-term investment decisions. By the end, you'll be equipped to evaluate whether a stock might be undervalued, fairly priced, or overvalued—and why.
