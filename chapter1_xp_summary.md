# Chapter 1 XP Distribution

## Subsection Quizzes
- 22 subsections × 75-100 XP each = ~1,800-2,200 XP

## Chapter Quiz
- Base completion: 1,000 XP
- "Foundations Master" achievement (80%+ score): 2,000 XP

## Total Possible XP from Chapter 1
- Approximately 4,800-5,200 XP

## Level Progression
- Level 1: 0 XP (starting level)
- Level 2: 1,000 XP
- Level 3: 5,000 XP
- Level 4: 10,000 XP
- Level 5: 20,000 XP
- Level 6: 40,000 XP
- Level 7: 80,000 XP
- Level 8: 150,000 XP
- Level 9: 400,000 XP
- Level 10: 1,000,000 XP

## XP Distribution Across Levels
- Levels 1-8: ~45% of total XP (450,000 XP)
- Level 9: ~25% of total XP (250,000 XP)
- Level 10: ~30% of total XP (300,000 XP)

This distribution ensures that users can progress through the early levels at a reasonable pace while still having significant challenges to overcome in the later levels.
